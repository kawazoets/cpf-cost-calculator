# CPF Cost Calculator Singapore

A simple web-based CPF cost calculator designed to visualize the real monthly employment cost in Singapore.

## Purpose

This tool helps Japanese HQ, local managers, HR, and finance teams understand the actual cost of hiring in Singapore.

It separates:

- Gross monthly salary
- Employee CPF
- Employer CPF
- Estimated net pay
- Total employer cost

## Why This Matters

In Singapore, the actual employment cost is not limited to gross salary.  
Employer CPF creates an additional cost that may not be immediately visible to overseas headquarters.

This tool is designed to bridge that cost perception gap.

## Scope

This calculator covers:

- Singapore Citizen
- SPR full rate
- Monthly Ordinary Wage
- 2026 CPF rates
- Private sector employment cost estimation

It does not cover:

- SPR first-year or second-year graduated rates
- Bonus or Additional Wage
- AW ceiling
- Partial month salary
- Special payroll cases

This tool provides an estimate only.
